Frequently Asked Questions ‚ùì

Get answers to common campus queries

The library is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and on weekends from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. During exams, extended hours are available until 10:00 PM.

Connect to the network "CampusNet" and use your student ID as username and registration number as password. For issues, contact the IT helpdesk at ext. 2345.

Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each course to be eligible for end-semester examinations. Medical leave with proper documentation is considered.

The weekly mess menu is posted on the hostel notice board every Sunday and is also available on the campus app under "Dining Services". Special dietary requirements can be requested at the mess office.

Event registrations are handled through the student portal. Log in with your credentials, navigate to "Events", and click "Register" on your desired event. Some events may have limited seats.

The sports complex is open from 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM daily. Gym facilities are available from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Equipment can be borrowed from the sports office with a valid student ID.
